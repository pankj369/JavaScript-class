Template Literals

  • Template :- literals are a way to use the variables in the string or a way to have embedded expressions in the string.

  • Eg :-(`The price of the ${obj.item} is ${obj.price} rupees.`)
    Output:- The price of the pen is 20 rupees.
    Another example:-(`The sum of the first 3 natural numbers is ${1+2+3}.`)
    Output:- The sum of the first 3 natural numbers is 6.

Escape Characters

  • Escape characters are used for next line, and it is denoted by \n.
    Here is an example:-(`This is a line.\nThis is a new line.`)
    Output:- This is a line.
    This is a new line.
